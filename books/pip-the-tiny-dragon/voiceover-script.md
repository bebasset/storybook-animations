# 🎙️ VOICE-OVER SCRIPT
## "Pip the Tiny Dragon with the Enormous Sneeze"

---
> **Tips before you start:**
> - Read slowly and warmly — pause at every "..." for effect
> - Use a BIG excited voice for action moments
> - Use a soft, sad voice for Pip's quiet moments
> - Make the ACHOO sound HUGE and silly
> - Kids love when you giggle through the funny parts!

---

### PAGE 1 — TITLE SCREEN
*(warm, inviting, story-time voice)*
"Once upon a time, in a magical place called Dragon Valley...
there lived a teeny, tiny little dragon...
named... **Pip.**"

*(pause, let them look at the animation)*

---

### PAGE 2 — MEET PIP
*(playful, cheerful)*
"Pip was the *smallest* dragon anyone in Dragon Valley had ever, EVER seen.

While the big dragons were big...
and tall...
and POWERFUL...

Pip was... well... little.

But he had the BIGGEST heart of them all."

---

### PAGE 3 — BIG DRAGONS BREATHE FIRE
*(dramatic, impressed)*
"The BIG dragons could breathe roaring, blazing, crackling FIRE!

They could light campfires from a mile away!
They could toast marshmallows in one second flat!
They could melt ice in the middle of winter!

But Pip?

*(pause — then tiny, soft voice)*
Pip could only make a tiny, little...
puff.

💨 ...puff.

Just a puff."

---

### PAGE 4 — ACHOO! SNEEZE CHAOS
*(build up slowly, then BURST)*
"Every time Pip tried to breathe fire...

*(sniffly, building voice)*
his nose would tingle...
his eyes would water...
his whole tiny body would scrunch up and...

*(SHOUT IT — make it silly and huge)*
**A — CHOOOOOO!!!** 💨

WHOOOOSH!
Hats went flying!
Leaves went spinning!
Birds went SQUAWKING off into the sky!
Umbrellas turned inside out!

*(giggle a little)*
Pip's sneeze was... something else."

---

### PAGE 5 — SORRY SORRY SORRY
*(apologetic, sheepish, a little sing-songy)*
"'Sorry! Sorry! SORRYYY!'

That's all Pip ever said after a sneeze.

He said sorry to the hat owners.
He said sorry to the birds.
He said sorry to the umbrella people.
He even said sorry to the LEAVES.

*(soft, honest)*
But he just... couldn't help it.
The sneezes always came."

---

### PAGE 6 — PIP FEELS LEFT OUT
*(gentle, quiet, a little sad — speak softly here)*
"Pip watched the big dragons do all their dragon things.

They breathed fire. They won trophies. They cheered for each other.

And Pip?
Pip sat on his little rock...
all by himself...
watching.

*(very quietly)*
'I'm useless,' he sighed.

A tiny little rain cloud seemed to follow him everywhere he went."

---

### PAGE 7 — THE GIANT SPIDER WEB
*(mysterious, spooky, slow)*
"Then one day...

*(hushed voice)*
a very strange...
very dark...
very SPOOKY thing happened.

A GIGANTIC magic spider web appeared out of nowhere!

*(dramatic)*
It stretched across the only bridge out of Dragon Valley.

A huge spider sat right at the top... grinning.

No one could get across.
Everyone was... STUCK."

---

### PAGE 8 — BIG DRAGONS TRY AND FAIL
*(building frustration, then defeated)*
"The biggest, strongest dragons in all of Dragon Valley marched up to that web.

They took deep, deep, DEEEEP breaths...

*(big dramatic pause)*

And they BLASTED it with their fiercest fire!

WHOOOOSH! 🔥🔥🔥

*(pause)*

...Nothing happened.

The web just... sat there.

Not even a single little thread was burned.

*(frustrated, defeated)*
The magic web wouldn't budge.
Not even one. Tiny. Bit."

---

### PAGE 9 — PIP'S NOSE TINGLES
*(slowly building, suspenseful)*
"Everyone looked at each other. No one knew what to do.

The big dragons crossed their arms.
They scratched their heads.
They shrugged their giant shoulders.

*(now focus on Pip)*
Then...

...everyone noticed something.

Pip's tiny nose...

*(whisper)*
...was tingling.

His eyes began to water.
His little ears started to wiggle.
His whole tiny body started to...
shake...
and shake...
and SHAKE...

*(voice getting bigger with each word)*
Oh no.
Oh nooo.
OH NOOOOO—"

---

### PAGE 10 — THE ENORMOUS SNEEZE
*(the biggest moment — GO FOR IT)*
"**A — CHOOOOOOOOOOOOO!!!!** 💨💨💨

THE BIGGEST...
THE LOUDEST...
THE MOST SPECTACULAR SNEEZE...
IN ALL OF DRAGON VALLEY!!!

The ground shook!
The trees bent sideways!
The birds in three kingdoms away said — 'What was THAT?!'

And that magic spider web?

*(pause for effect)*

...it was GONE.

Just like that.

*(quietly awed)*
Gone."

---

### PAGE 11 — WEB IS GONE
*(joyful, wonder-filled)*
"Where the web once was...

...there was nothing but clear, open, beautiful bridge.

A rainbow appeared in the sky.

Every dragon turned...
and stared...
at tiny little Pip.

Their mouths hung open.
Their eyes went WIDE.

*(whisper)*
Had Pip really just done that?"

---

### PAGE 12 — CELEBRATION
*(pure joy — this is the fun page!)*
"AND THEN —

'HOORAY FOR PIP!!!' everyone cheered!

The dragons jumped!
They stomped their feet!
They waved their wings!
Confetti flew through the air!

*(big, proud voice)*
'THE BEST SNEEZER IN ALL THE LAND!!!'

They lifted tiny little Pip up high...
and gave him the biggest dragon trophy
anyone had ever seen.

*(laugh warmly)*
Pip couldn't believe it.
He had SAVED Dragon Valley...
with a sneeze."

---

### PAGE 13 — THE END
*(warm, slow, meaningful — let it land)*
"From that day on...

Pip never said sorry for his sneeze again.

Not once.

Because he finally understood something very, very important.

*(slowly, with feeling)*
The thing that makes you different...

...is not something to be sorry about.

The thing that makes you different...

...might be exactly what the world needs.

*(soft and warm)*
So whatever YOUR special thing is —
your giggle, your wobble, your sneeze —

don't ever be sorry for it.

It's a gift. 🎁

*(final beat)*
...The End. 🐉✨"

---

## 🎬 RECORDING TIPS

| Scene | Tone |
|-------|------|
| Title / Intro | Warm, inviting |
| Meet Pip | Light and sweet |
| Fire scenes | Excited, dramatic |
| Sneezes | BIG and silly |
| Sad scenes | Gentle, slow |
| Spider web | Spooky, hushed |
| THE SNEEZE | Loudest moment — commit to it! |
| Celebration | Pure joy |
| The End | Slow, meaningful, loving |

**Pacing:** The whole book should take about **6–8 minutes** at a relaxed read-aloud pace.
