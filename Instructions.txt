=====================================================
  ANIMATED STORYBOOKS - INSTRUCTIONS FOR MY SISTER
  (No tech experience needed - I promise it's easy!)
=====================================================

Hey Sis! 🎉

I made you animated storybooks you can use for your class
and YouTube channel. Follow these steps and you'll be
up and running in about 10 minutes!


=====================================================
STEP 1 — CREATE A FREE GITHUB ACCOUNT
=====================================================

GitHub is just a website that stores files. Think of it
like Google Drive but for projects. It's completely free.

1. Go to:  https://github.com
2. Click "Sign up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email, create a password, and pick a username
4. Verify your email when they send you a confirmation
5. That's it — you have an account!


=====================================================
STEP 2 — DOWNLOAD THE STORYBOOKS TO YOUR COMPUTER
=====================================================

Now you'll grab all the files from my page:

1. Go to:  https://github.com/bebasset/storybook-animations
2. Look for the green button that says "< > Code"
   (it's near the top right of the page)
3. Click it — a small box will pop up
4. Click "Download ZIP" at the bottom of that box
5. A file called "storybook-animations-main.zip" will
   download to your computer (check your Downloads folder)
6. Find that ZIP file and double-click it to unzip it
7. You'll now have a folder called "storybook-animations-main"
   — move it somewhere easy to find, like your Desktop


=====================================================
STEP 3 — OPEN A STORYBOOK
=====================================================

1. Open the "storybook-animations-main" folder
2. Open the "books" folder inside it
3. Open the "pip-the-tiny-dragon" folder
4. Double-click the file called "animation.html"
   — it will open in your web browser automatically!
5. You'll see the animated title screen

To turn pages:
   → Click the "Next" button on screen
   OR press the right arrow key ( → ) on your keyboard

To go back:
   → Click the "Back" button
   OR press the left arrow key ( ← )


=====================================================
STEP 4 — READ THE VOICEOVER SCRIPT
=====================================================

Inside the same "pip-the-tiny-dragon" folder there is
a file called "voiceover-script.md"

You can open it with:
   - Notepad (Windows)
   - TextEdit (Mac)
   - Or any word processor

It has the full script — everything you say for each page,
plus tips on tone (when to be silly, dramatic, soft, etc.)

Print it out so you can read it while the animation plays!


=====================================================
STEP 5 — RECORD IT FOR YOUTUBE (OPTIONAL)
=====================================================

To record yourself reading over the animation:

OPTION A — Screen Recorder (easiest)
   Windows: Press  Windows key + G  to open Xbox Game Bar
            Then click the record button
   Mac:     Press  Command + Shift + 5  for screen recording

OPTION B — OBS Studio (best quality, free)
   Download at:  https://obsproject.com
   It records your screen AND your voice at the same time
   YouTube has great beginner tutorials — just search
   "OBS tutorial for beginners"

OPTION C — Record on your phone
   Set your phone up pointing at the screen and just
   hit record on your phone camera. Simple and it works!


=====================================================
WHEN NEW BOOKS ARE ADDED
=====================================================

I'll email you whenever I add a new book. To get it:

1. Go back to:  https://github.com/bebasset/storybook-animations
2. Click the green "< > Code" button again
3. Click "Download ZIP" again
4. Unzip it and you'll have all the books including the new one!

(No need to delete the old folder — just replace it with
the new download)
